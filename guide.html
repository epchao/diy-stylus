Step-By-Step Guide: How to make the DIY Stylus

btw: this pen can only be used for minimal tasks such as surfing the web.

Materials

Pencil
Aluminum Foil
Pencil Eraser
Scissors
Tape
Tablet or any mobile device

Guide: How to make the DIY Stylus

Step One: Press eraser inside the pencil tip.

Grab a small eraser and press the eraser inside the pencil tip, so that the eraser is secured tightly inside the tip of the pencil.

Step Two: Wrap aluminum foil around the eraser.

Grab some aluminum foil and cut it into smaller pieces if needed and then wrap it around the eraser until it covers the bottom and the sides of it and also a portion of the pencil. Make sure to press firmly, so that the aluminum foil doesn't come off of the eraser.

Step Three: Place small pieces of tape around the top of the eraser

Grab some tape and cut small pieces and place them around the top of the eraser (on the side where the eraser meets the pencil) so that the eraser doesn't come off of the pencil.

Step Four: Enjoy your new stylus!

Your DIY Stylus is now complete! To use the pen, make sure one of your fingers is touching the aluminum foil because of sciency stuff.
